Our Proprietary Diagnosis System

Balmers developed and copyright-registered a diagnostic framework
that analyzes hair loss from the perspective of body heat imbalance.

🔥

Scalp Heat Syndrome

두피열 (頭皮熱)

Excessive heat concentrates on the scalp, weakening hair follicles. Stress, sleep deprivation, and overwork cause body heat imbalance, widening pores and shortening the hair growth cycle.

↔️

Upper Heat-Lower Cold

상열하한 (上熱下寒)

A constitutional imbalance where the upper body overheats while the lower body stays cold. Based on the principle that "a cool head and warm abdomen means good health," correcting this improves the scalp environment.

💫

Liver Qi Stagnation

간기울결 (肝氣鬱結)

Stress blocks the circulation of Qi (vital energy). Chronic stress causes disruptions in blood and energy circulation, which is one of the primary causes of hair loss.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Traditional Korean Medicine effective for hair loss?
TKM treats hair loss by correcting underlying internal imbalances. Balmers has specialized for over 15 years with 1.88M+ treatments. Hair regrowth is possible when follicles are still active. Results vary by individual.
How is TKM different from Western medicine?
Western medicine uses medications (finasteride, minoxidil) or surgery. TKM uses natural therapies — acupuncture, herbal medicine, pharmacopuncture — to restore overall balance without pharmaceutical side effects.
How long does treatment take?
Standard program is 6 months with weekly visits. Duration varies by progression. International patients can receive intensive treatment during their stay, continued remotely after returning home.
Can I receive treatment without speaking Korean?
Yes. Balmers provides English, Chinese, and Japanese consultations. Interpretation services arranged on advance booking. Reports provided in your preferred language.
Are there side effects from herbal medicine?
Herbal medicines are natural and individually prescribed. Side effects are generally minimal, though temporary digestive issues may occur. Pre-treatment constitutional analysis minimizes risk.
What makes Balmers different?
Balmers is Korea's only single-brand hair loss clinic since 2009. 20 clinics with standardized protocols. Copyright-registered proprietary systems (Body Heat Regulation Theory, BHT, BAP). 3 books, 10+ papers published.
