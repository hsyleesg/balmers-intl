Our Proprietary Diagnosis System

Balmers has developed and copyright-registered its own diagnostic framework
that analyzes hair loss from the perspective of body heat imbalance.

🔥

Scalp Heat Syndrome

두피열 (頭皮熱)

A condition where excessive heat concentrates on the scalp, weakening hair follicles. Stress, sleep deprivation, and overwork cause body heat imbalance, which widens pores and shortens the hair growth cycle.

↔️

Upper Heat-Lower Cold Imbalance

상열하한 (上熱下寒)

A constitutional imbalance where the upper body overheats while the lower body stays cold. Based on the Korean medicine principle that "a cool head and warm abdomen means good health," correcting this imbalance improves the scalp environment.

💫

Liver Qi Stagnation

간기울결 (肝氣鬱結)

A condition where stress blocks the circulation of Qi (vital energy). Chronic stress causes disruptions in blood and energy circulation, which is one of the primary causes of hair loss.

Treatment Methods

Core Korean Medicine Therapies

We combine acupuncture, herbal medicine, pharmacopuncture, and scalp care.

Herbal Medicine

한약 (Hanyak)

Individually prescribed based on each patient's constitution and hair loss cause. Aimed at restoring circulation and strengthening adrenal function to correct body heat imbalance.

Acupuncture

침 치료 (Chim Chiryo)

Regulates meridians to improve overall blood and energy circulation. Uses a combination of points for kidney strengthening, circulation promotion, and scalp heat reduction.

Pharmacopuncture

약침 (Yakchim)

Purified herbal medicine ingredients are injected directly into the affected area. Helps stabilize immune responses and reduce scalp heat sensation.

Scalp Care

두피 관리 (Dupi Gwanri)

Herbal topical treatments improve the scalp environment. MTS micro-needling enhances active ingredient absorption, complemented by daily care with herbal-infused products.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Is Traditional Korean Medicine effective for hair loss?
Traditional Korean Medicine (TKM) treats hair loss by correcting the underlying internal imbalances that cause it. Balmers Korean Medicine Clinic has specialized exclusively in hair loss treatment for over 15 years, with more than 1.88 million cumulative treatments. Hair regrowth is possible when hair follicles are still active. Treatment outcomes vary depending on the individual's condition.
How is TKM hair loss treatment different from Western medicine?
Western medicine primarily treats hair loss with medications (finasteride, minoxidil) or hair transplant surgery. Traditional Korean Medicine uses natural therapies — acupuncture, herbal medicine, and pharmacopuncture — to restore the body's overall balance. The key advantage is treating the root cause without concerns about pharmaceutical side effects.
How long does the treatment take?
The standard treatment program is 6 months, with weekly clinic visits. Total treatment duration may vary depending on individual hair loss progression. For international patients on short visits, intensive treatment during the stay can be continued with remote follow-up care after returning home.
Can I receive treatment if I don't speak Korean?
Yes. Balmers provides consultation services in English, Chinese, and Japanese. Interpretation services are arranged upon advance reservation. Diagnosis reports and treatment plans are also provided in the patient's preferred language.
Are there side effects from herbal medicine?
Herbal medicines are composed of natural ingredients and are individually prescribed based on each patient's constitution. Side effects are generally minimal, though temporary digestive issues may occur depending on the specific herbs used. Pre-treatment constitutional analysis minimizes the risk of adverse reactions.
